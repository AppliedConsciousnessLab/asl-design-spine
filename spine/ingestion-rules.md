# Ingestion Rules

Ingestion introduces candidates, not truth.

---

## Allowed Sources
- ZIP exports
- Documents (docx, pdf)
- Notes (Samsung Notes)
- Human-authored text

---

## Rules
- Raw ingestion is non-canonical
- No ingestion mutates canon
- All ingested items are classified as candidates

---

## Promotion
Promotion requires:
1. Human intent
2. Canon compatibility
3. Explicit approval
4. Version association

Automation may propose.
Humans decide.
